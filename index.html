Below is a selection of professional projects on which I was (or am) the Lead Engineer. In many cases, I am directly responsible for >=80% of the code. If not, I'll mention it!

To start with, here's a sample of a fun project I worked on a couple years ago. Think Samsung SmartPause.

Connected Device

Kindara Wink - Accurate and precise Bluetooth LE thermometer automatically syncs temperatures to Kindara’s Android and iOS apps. As the Lead Engineer at Kindara, I am directly responsible for the success of Wink, re-engineering the Embedded C firmware and iOS CoreBluetooth API, and completely scrapping the Android BLE API. This is my proudest achievement as an engineer - I had barely written any C or C++ since university besides some NDK work on Android, and never written Embedded C and re-wrote 90% of the firmware in under six months after being hired as a senior Android resource. While simultaneously handling the BLE integrations for both of Kindara's apps. Whew!

Android

Aerovironment ProCore - provides technicians an in-depth Bluetooth LE interface to ProCore charging stations to charge and configure industrial electric forklifts.

Samantha - App suite and SDK associates Android sensor data at 50hz, touch interaction, and packets collected from a Bluetooth LE GSR sensor. This project grew from humble roots to demonstrate a BLE integration on Android to a suite of apps geared for researchers to understand how users interact with mobile devices. Data is marshalled in real-time to SQLite and on to Google BigQuery in a multithreaded environment - the biggest challenge is how to handle data being generated at a rate that even high end Android devices could barely tolerate.

SaveIt- Archive and search texts, voicemails, and photos with this cloud-connected app and RESTFul server platform that provides custom voicemail greetings and recordings via Plivo.

Zymurgy - The American Hoymebrewer Association’s app provides seamless online->offline access and searching capabilities to the entire PDF archive of AHA’s flagship publication, Zymurgy.

iOS

IDB GalleryX- Award-winning iPad app presents a virtual gallery of the Inter-American Development Bank’s art collection, served from a RESTful Web Api.

Web

E-LRNX - Electronic learning system provides a Web 2.0 interface to the World Bank’s operational lessons database, complete with Lucene full-text indexing and geographic search functions.
